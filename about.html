About Us

Unreal Authorized Training Center

We are now an Unreal Authorized Training Center (UATC). As a UATC, we are committed to helping developers improve their skills and knowledge of the Unreal Engine. We offer a range of trainings and courses designed to help professionals and hobbyists alike learn how to effectively use the Unreal Engine to create stunning games and interactive experiences.

Our courses are led by experienced instructors who have a deep understanding of the platform and its capabilities. They combine lectures, hands‑on exercises and real‑world projects to help students learn the tools and techniques they need to succeed. We also offer customised training programmes for companies and organisations looking to upskill their teams or get started with Unreal Engine.

Company Info

Athian Games is a game development studio founded in 2018 with a focus on creating high‑end, story‑based games using the Unreal Engine. We are dedicated to delivering immersive and engaging gaming experiences to players around the world. Our mission is to create games that are not only enjoyable to play, but also offer meaningful and memorable experiences.

We believe Unreal Engine is the best platform for creating cutting‑edge games that push the boundaries of what is possible. It offers a wide range of tools and features that enable us to create highly detailed and realistic environments and characters, as well as smooth and seamless gameplay mechanics. We are constantly exploring new and innovative ways to use the engine to build the best possible gaming experiences for our players.
